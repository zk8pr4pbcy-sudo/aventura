Before you book

Reservation timing

Booking 2–3 days ahead is preferred. Next-day arrangements may be possible subject to availability.

02

Payment

A 25% deposit confirms the reservation. The remaining balance is due 48 hours before departure.

03

Marine conditions

If weather or a boat fault prevents sailing, the trip is rescheduled or refunded when rescheduling is not possible.
