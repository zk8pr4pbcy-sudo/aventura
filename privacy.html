Privacy policy

A clear summary of the information we receive and how it is used.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information you provide

When you contact us, you may provide your name, phone number, email, company, preferred date, guest count and details of the requested experience, event or service.

2. How we use information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, prepare proposals, coordinate requested services, maintain business records and improve our guest communication.

3. Sharing with service partners

When necessary to prepare or deliver an approved request, relevant details may be shared with selected providers such as guides, venues, transport companies or hospitality partners. We share only what is reasonably needed for that purpose.

4. Website preferences

The website may store your language preference on your device. We do not currently use this website to take online payments or create customer accounts.

5. WhatsApp and email

If you continue through WhatsApp or email, your communication is also subject to the privacy practices of that service provider.

6. Data retention and protection

We retain inquiry and service information only as needed for business, contractual and legal purposes, and apply reasonable measures to protect it from unauthorized access.

7. Your choices

You may ask us to correct or delete information you previously submitted, subject to any information we must retain for legal or contractual reasons.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact amassiri@aventuraksa.com.
