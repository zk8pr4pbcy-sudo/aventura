Frequently asked questions

A clear starting point before we tailor the details around your request.

How do I book an experience?

Send the preferred date, guest count and interests through the request form or WhatsApp. We will confirm availability and provide the proposed program before any payment.

Are experiences private?

Our core programs are designed for private guests and groups. If a shared option is ever suggested, it will be stated clearly before confirmation.

Can you combine several experiences in one day?

Yes. We balance travel time, weather and guest pace so a combined day remains enjoyable rather than rushed.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes. Private transportation can be added according to group size, route and required service level.

Are licensed guides available in different languages?

Yes, subject to availability. English and Arabic are commonly available, and additional languages can be requested in advance.

Can Aventura organize corporate events?

Yes. We coordinate meetings, conferences, launches, team programs, employee occasions and visiting delegation programs.

Do you publish fixed prices online?

Some services may have a standard starting point, but most programs depend on the date, group, duration and inclusions. A written quotation is shared before confirmation.

Can products be delivered to a hotel?

Yes. Welcome boxes, gifts and selected personal services can be coordinated for hotel or accommodation delivery by prior arrangement.

How early should I contact you?

Earlier is better, especially for weekends, larger groups and specialist guides. Short-notice requests can still be checked based on availability.

What happens after I send a request?

We review the details, ask any necessary questions, propose a direction and confirm availability. Nothing is booked until you approve the final scope and terms.

Tell us what you want the day to feel like.

Share the occasion, group size and preferred date. We will shape a clear plan around your guests and priorities.
